Omelette

Description

A simple omelette dish that can be made really quickly without too much of items requirements

Ingredients

  • 1 or 2 medium sized egg(s)
  • 1 tablespoon of cut tomato
  • 1 tablespoon of cut onion
  • 1 or 2 tablespoon of Cooking oil
  • some salt

Steps

  1. Crack eggs in a bowl
  2. Add tomato and onions to it
  3. Mix until the eggs are fully converted to yellow mixture
  4. Add cooking oil to frying pan and let it heat for a bit
  5. After 1 or 2 minutes of wait add the paste in it frying pan
  6. Add some salt in the paste as well
  7. Wait for 1 or 2 minutes and flip the omelette
  8. If its not cooked well dont worry you can flip it again after cooking the other side
  9. When you see its brown enough or cooked enough turn off the gas and server the dish
  10. Voila your dish is ready to eat
