Read Beans

Description

A simple recipe to make red bean curry. This dish takes around 1 to 2 hours but is satisfying to serve.

Ingredients

  • 200 grams (or however much you like to eat curry) of red beans ofc
  • 6 to 7 garlic cloves
  • 2 medium sized tomato or 3 small ones
  • Some cooking oil
  • some spices if you want your curry spicy

Steps

  1. Boil the red beans in a cooker for around 45 mins
  2. Cut garlic cloves in small pieces
  3. Cut tomato in small pieces or blend them in juicer
  4. Take some cooking oil and add them in cooking pot
  5. Add garlic to it and wait until garlic is brownish
  6. Add tomato to the cooking pot (in this step you can add spices as well if you like it)
  7. stir the wooden spoon or any kind of spoon in the mix for around 2 to 3 minutes
  8. Add the red beans from the cooker to the pot and add 1 small cup of water in it
  9. Stir for around 1 minute and add some more water in it (if you want your curry thick i recommend adding less water)
  10. Let it cook for around 5 to 10 minutes until the water is fully red browninsh
  11. Voila your dish is served
