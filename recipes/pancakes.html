Pancakes

Description

A simple pancake recipe that can be readied within 5 mins to 10 mins

Ingredients (for 1 person serving)

  • 1 cup of pancake poweder
  • 3 tablespoon of oil
  • 1 medium sized egg or 2 small eggs
  • 2/3 cup of milk (add more if needed based on thickness of paste)
  • sugar if you want to eat without syrup or something

Steps

  1. Make a pancake paste by adding all the above items and mixing them
  2. Add 1 or 2 tablespoon of cooking oil on frying pan
  3. Add 1/2 cup of paste to frying pan and wait for the bubbles to form
  4. Flip the pancake after 1 minute or so (be sure to keep an eye out to not burn the other side of the pancake)
  5. Wait another 1 minute and voila your pancake is now ready to eat or serve
